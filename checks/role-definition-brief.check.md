# Role Definition Brief Check

## Purpose

Validate that a role definition brief is explicit enough to support design of the role's artifacts, workflows, checks, and supporting skills.

## Applies To

- instantiated copies of [`artifacts/role-definition-brief.md`](D:/Projects/agoge/artifacts/role-definition-brief.md)
- Use after the initial role definition has been drafted.
- Do not use as a substitute for reviewing the full support package.

## Criteria

- The role name and job-to-be-done are explicit.
- The intended audience or downstream consumers are identified.
- The role's lifecycle position is clear.
- Success criteria are present.
- Primary responsibilities are separated from out-of-scope responsibilities.
- Default outputs are stated clearly enough to drive the support-system design.
- Source references, assumptions, and open questions are visible.

## Scoring Or Outcome

Pass/fail.

The artifact passes only if another designer could use it to build the role's support package without inventing the role's purpose or boundaries.

## Evidence Required

- The role definition brief.
- Any source references or comparison notes needed to verify that the role is grounded in real inputs.

If evidence is missing or the role's job is unclear, fail the check and identify the undefined sections.

## Supporting Skills

- [`role-definition`](D:/Projects/agoge/skills/role-definition/SKILL.md) when the role brief needs to be reworked into a clearer job-to-be-done, boundary, and output definition.
- [`meeting-notes-and-actions`](D:/Projects/agoge/skills/meeting-notes-and-actions/SKILL.md) when workshop notes, interview notes, or meeting transcripts need to be normalized into candidate responsibilities, boundaries, assumptions, and open questions before the brief can be reworked cleanly.
- [`research-documentation`](D:/Projects/agoge/skills/research-documentation/SKILL.md) when the role definition still depends on multiple local references that need synthesis.
- [`content-research-writer`](D:/Projects/agoge/skills/content-research-writer/SKILL.md) when external references or comparison material need to be summarized before the role can be defined clearly.

## Failure Response

- Rework the role definition brief before designing the rest of the package.
- Do not derive artifacts, workflows, or checks from a role whose purpose or boundaries are still vague.

## Notes

This is the first quality gate for role-package design. If it fails, the rest of the package is likely to inherit confusion.
