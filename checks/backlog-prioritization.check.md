# Backlog Prioritization Check

## Purpose

Validate that the backlog prioritization artifact makes the current ordering, rationale, acceptance-oriented conditions, deferred scope, and reprioritization triggers explicit.

## Applies To

- Instantiated copies of [`backlog-prioritization.md`](D:/Projects/orpheum/artifacts/backlog-prioritization.md)
- Use before treating current priorities as ready for downstream solutioning, planning, or delivery decisions

## Criteria

- The prioritization scope is explicit.
- Prioritized work is listed in a clear order.
- Ordering rationale is tied to value, urgency, learning, risk, or constraint signals rather than to vague preference.
- Acceptance-oriented conditions and guardrails are explicit where they materially affect ordering.
- Deferred or excluded scope is visible.
- Major sequencing assumptions, dependencies, and stakeholder tensions are recorded.
- Reprioritization triggers are explicit.

## Scoring Or Outcome

Pass/fail.

## Evidence Required

- The instantiated backlog prioritization artifact
- The product direction artifact and supporting inputs that justify the ordering

## Supporting Skills

- [`requirements-verification`](D:/Projects/orpheum/skills/requirements-verification/SKILL.md) when prioritization hinges on validated business or user commitments.
- [`research-documentation`](D:/Projects/orpheum/skills/research-documentation/SKILL.md) when tradeoffs or evidence are scattered across local notes.

## Failure Response

- Rework the prioritization artifact before treating it as a downstream-ready product ordering decision.
- Route unresolved tradeoffs or discovery gaps back to the earliest source that can clarify them.
